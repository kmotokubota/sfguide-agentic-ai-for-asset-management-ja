## Template Purpose

This template provides structure for welcoming new institutional clients and guiding them through the onboarding process. It establishes clear expectations, introduces key contacts, and ensures a smooth transition into the SAM client experience.

**Audience**: Newly onboarded institutional clients during their first 90 days
